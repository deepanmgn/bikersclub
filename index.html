India's first bikers club
Based in Gachibowli, Hyderabad.

INTRO

Operating from the Gachibowli area, Bikers club is the first club to include like-minded biker enthusiasts. Started in Oct 2015 by two enthusiasts, our club involves frolic and fun to travel on the open road.

FUN

We hold many events during the weekends where we go on long rides at stunning beautiful places. We also arrange food and catering for on the go events and also get to spend our nights like hikers.

FRIENDS

You are more than welcome to bring your friends along with you. You need not necessarily get two bikes as the person can ride in pillion. Some events may take upto a day or two, so we advise you to be prepared. You will have to bring your own camping gear.

REQUIREMENTS

Being in a biker's club, we do expect you to have a bike of your own although we can arrange bike rentals for a nominal fee. The membership fee is Rs.300/- per month as we do need some form of funding to keep the club alive. We do arrange special discounts if you bring in more persons.
